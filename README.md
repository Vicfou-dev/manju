## What is Manju?

Manju translates manga, webtoons, comics, and doujinshi into your language — instantly, in the browser tab you're already in. Drop in an image, a full chapter, or a batch of pages, and get clean, typeset translations back in seconds. No install, no server round-trip for your images, no account required to try it.

Under the hood it runs OCR, panel/bubble detection, inpainting, and translation entirely client-side — your pages never leave your device.

<br>

## ✨ Features

- 🖼️ **Any image, any source** — manga, webtoons, western comics, and doujinshi (18+)
- 🔒 **Privacy by design** — the full translation pipeline runs in your browser; nothing is uploaded to a server
- 📚 **Batch translation** — drop in a whole chapter as a multi-page PDF or CBZ/ZIP
- 🔎 **Manga chapter search** — find and translate a chapter without hunting for raw images first
- 🌍 **19 languages** — English, Français, Deutsch, Español, Italiano, 日本語, 한국어, 中文 (简体 & 繁體), Русский, Türkçe, Tiếng Việt, ไทย, Polski, Nederlands, Português, हिंदी, Bahasa Indonesia, Українська
- 🏆 **Leaderboard & levels** — earn XP for every page you translate and climb the global ranking
- 🎬 **Free forever tier** — 5 free translations to start, plus more anytime by watching a short ad

## 🔒 Privacy-first

Most manga translators ship your images to a server to do the OCR/translation work. Manju doesn't — panel detection, text recognition, inpainting, and rendering all happen on-device, powered by WebAssembly and in-browser ML models. Your pages stay yours.
